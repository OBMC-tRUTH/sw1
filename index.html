MONTHLY TOP ARTISTS

Known for their powerful lyrics, unique sound, and ability to capture complex emotions in their music. From heartfelt ballads to energetic anthems, they effortlessly blend different genres, creating music that resonates with personal experiences of love, growth, and self-discovery. Whether through introspective storytelling or bold, genre-defying experimentation, these artists have carved out a space for themselves in the music world, and their songs always feel like a reflection of my own thoughts and feelings.

FAVORITE ARTISTS

NIKI

NIKI is an Indonesian singer, songwriter, and producer known for her smooth blend of R&B, pop, and soul influences. Rising to fame as part of the 88rising collective, she captivates with her emotive vocals, introspective lyrics, and genre-defying sound. NIKI's music explores themes of identity, love, and self-discovery, making her a distinctive voice in the global music scene.

TAYLOR SWIFT

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter renowned for her versatile music that spans country, pop, and indie genres. Known for her heartfelt lyrics and storytelling, she has become one of the best-selling artists of all time. Swift’s ability to evolve with each album, coupled with her personal connection to her fans, has made her a cultural icon, with numerous Grammy Awards and record-breaking achievements to her name.

OLIVIA RODRIGO

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress who skyrocketed to fame with her debut album SOUR in 2021. Known for her raw, confessional lyrics and powerful vocals, she blends pop, alt-rock, and pop-punk influences, capturing the angst and emotion of adolescence. Olivia’s breakout hit “drivers license” became a global sensation, establishing her as one of the most influential young artists of her generation.

SABRINA CARPENTER

Sabrina Carpenter is an American singer, songwriter, and actress known for her pop-leaning sound and versatile talent. Rising to fame through Disney Channel's Girl Meets World, she transitioned to music with a string of successful albums and catchy singles. With her empowering lyrics and blend of pop, indie, and folk influences, Sabrina has earned a dedicated fanbase and critical acclaim for her growth as an artist.

FAVORITE ALBUMS

WANNA TAKE THIS DOWNTOWN

WANNA TAKE THIS DOWNTOWN is the debut album by Indonesian-American singer-songwriter NIKI, released in 2023. The album blends elements of R&B, pop, and indie, showcasing NIKI's soulful voice and deeply personal songwriting. With themes of love, longing, and self-discovery, *WANNA TAKE THIS DOWNTOWN* takes listeners on an emotional journey, exploring both the highs and lows of relationships and personal growth. NIKI's smooth vocals, paired with atmospheric production, create a captivating and intimate listening experience that highlights her unique artistry.

REPUTATION

Reputation is Taylor Swift's sixth studio album, released in 2017. The album marks a departure from her previous country and pop styles, embracing darker, synth-driven production and themes of love, betrayal, and media scrutiny. With hits like "Look What You Made Me Do" and "Delicate," Swift navigates her evolving public image and personal relationships, blending bold reinvention with vulnerability. Reputation is a testament to her resilience and ability to turn controversy into art.

GUTS

GUTS is Olivia Rodrigo's highly anticipated sophomore album, released in 2023. Building on the raw emotional intensity of her debut, SOUR, GUTS explores themes of growing up, self-discovery, heartbreak, and frustration. With a mix of introspective ballads and angsty pop-punk anthems, Rodrigo channels her experiences into a deeply personal and powerful collection. The album showcases her evolving sound, blending confessional lyrics with bold experimentation, solidifying her place as one of the most influential young voices in pop music.

SINGULAR: ACT 1

SINGULAR: ACT 1 is the debut solo album by singer-songwriter Sabrina Carpenter, released in 2018. With a mix of pop, R&B, and electronic influences, the album showcases her growth as an artist, blending catchy hooks with introspective lyrics. Themes of self-discovery, independence, and love run throughout the project, offering a more mature and nuanced side of Carpenter compared to her previous work. *SINGULAR: ACT 1* is a confident step into new musical territory, with standout tracks like "Almost Love" and "Sue Me" demonstrating her evolving sound and bold artistic vision.

FAVORITE SONGS

URS

"urs" by NIKI is a tender, heartfelt ballad about the yearning for deep, unconditional love. Through intimate lyrics and gentle melodies, NIKI expresses vulnerability and desire for a connection that transcends time and distance. The song captures the bittersweet feeling of longing, evoking the intensity of wanting to be entirely devoted to someone. With its soulful vocals and dreamy production, "urs" is a raw and emotional exploration of love and loyalty.

DRESS

"DRESS" by Taylor Swift is a sultry, confessional track that dives into the depths of secret attraction and desire. With whispery vocals and a seductive beat, Swift captures the tension of a love that exists under wraps, highlighting the thrill of intimacy and vulnerability. The lyrics reveal both a physical and emotional longing, celebrating the private moments shared between two people and the powerful pull of hidden romance.

VAMPIRE

"VAMPIRE" by Olivia Rodrigo is a fierce and haunting anthem about betrayal and heartbreak. In this raw, emotionally charged track, Rodrigo explores the pain of realizing someone’s true intentions after giving them her trust. With a dark, moody melody and powerful vocals, she compares her toxic relationship to being drained by a vampire, emphasizing feelings of disillusionment and loss. The song’s biting lyrics and dramatic sound capture the intensity of heartbreak and the process of reclaiming self-worth.

SUE ME

"SUE ME" by Sabrina Carpenter is an empowering, upbeat anthem about self-confidence and resilience after a breakup. With sassy lyrics and catchy beats, Carpenter addresses her critics and ex, declaring her right to move on unapologetically. The song’s playful attitude and bold delivery showcase her determination to live life on her own terms, making it a feel-good track about embracing independence and brushing off negativity.
